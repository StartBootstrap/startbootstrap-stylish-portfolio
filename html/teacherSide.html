Prepare Your Class

This activity is designed to help your students understand the flow of a short story. To use this activity, paste a story of your own choosing into the box below and then press the "Randomize Short Story" button. The program will separate the story into sentences, randomize the sentences, and provide you with a link to share with your students. When your students access that link they will be able to drag each sentence with the goal of re-creating the full story.

Note: Please ensure that sentences are separated by a "." in order for this program to work correctly.

Reading: Literature Standard 5

Get Started

Materials:



For the Student:

  • Several different shaped magnets
  • 15-20 small paper clips
  • Science Journal





STEP 1

Instructions



Magnetism can be used to hold some objects together in a chain.






STEP 2

Instructions




  1. Examine all the different types of magnets available.
  2. Predict which magnet will pick up the most paper clips and why. Record their prediction and reason in your Science Journal.
  3. Use one or more magnets to make a paper clip chain with each magnet.
  4. Record the number of paper clips different magnets pick up in a chain in your Science Journals.
  5. Finish experimenting with the magnet you were given, you should trade magnets with another student until you have tried them all.
  6. Sort and sequence data according to criteria given.







STEP 3

Instructions



Use data to construct a reasonable conclusion.







STEP 4

Instructions



Seek and weigh evidence before drawing conclusions.


Discuss the results.

Questions
